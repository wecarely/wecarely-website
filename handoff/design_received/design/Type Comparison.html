My take

I'd ship Spectral for the listing.

The Karelia reference reads luxurious because it's a hero spread for tourism. Houston home care needs the same elegance but with clinical credibility — a daughter scanning ten agencies at midnight has to read them, not admire them. Spectral keeps the modern serif charm and adds the weight range and on-screen sharpness Tenor Sans and Cormorant lack.

When to pick the others
  • Tenor Sans — if WeCarely is going to lean editorial / brand-magazine, with photo-led marketing pages alongside the listing.
  • Cormorant Garamond — if the brand wants a softer, more fashion-adjacent feel; ideal if you ever do a print zine of family stories.
  • Spectral — the safe, modern, fintech-trustworthy default. Best for a directory product that lives or dies on legibility.
